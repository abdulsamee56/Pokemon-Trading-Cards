Pokemon Trading Cards

This Tkinter-based application presents a card collection game where users can open packs of virtual cards and manage their collection. Upon launching the game, users are provided with options to open card packs and view their collected cards. The application offers an engaging experience for virtual card collecting, allowing users to explore and expand their collection within the game environment.
